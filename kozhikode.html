KOZHIKODE

The largest city of Kerala, Calicut is currently known as Kozhikode. Founded in the year 1948 by Vasco Da Gama, it lies on the south west coast of Arabian Sea. Largely popular for its alluring charm and picturesque views, Calicut is indeed one of the most prominent tourist attractions of Kerala. If you read through the history of Calicut, you’ll know that the place has been under the rule of several dynasties. It has always been an opulent trading town and is an erstwhile capital of the daunting Zamorin dynasty. Another significant fact about Calicut is that its name comes from Calico which is a hand woven cloth. Calicut indeed has innumerable things to attract tourists
